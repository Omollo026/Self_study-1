Floating elements

Human capital theory is not only critical for ensuring the health and success of the organization but to enable you to compete in an increasingly competitive world. Prioritizing it will enable an organization attract ,retain and engage the talented, high performing people it needs to be successful , from finding the right talent to onboarding and supporting the employee experience throughout the employee life cycle.
